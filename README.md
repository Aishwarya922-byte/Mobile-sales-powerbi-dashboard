Mobile Sales Dashboard – Power BI

An interactive Mobile Sales Dashboard built in Microsoft Power BI to analyze sales performance across brands, models, months, and payment methods. This dashboard provides actionable insights to help businesses monitor sales trends, customer preferences, and transactions.

Features
Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)

Total Sales: 14.35M

Total Quantity Sold: 389

Total Transactions: 77

Average Sales per Transaction: 37K

Dashboard Visuals

Total Sales by Brand: Line chart showing sales for brands like Xiaomi, OnePlus, Apple, Vivo, and Samsung.

Total Quantity by Month and Day: Visualizes quantity fluctuations across days within a month.

Total Sales by Day Name: Weekly sales trend across Saturday, Sunday, Monday, and other weekdays.

Total Sales by Mobile Model: Horizontal bar chart comparing models such as Vivo Y51, Vivo S1, and Vivo V20.

Customer Ratings: Funnel chart illustrating distribution of customer ratings.

Count of Transactions by Payment Method: Pie chart showing transaction distribution across UPI, Debit Card, Cash, etc.
